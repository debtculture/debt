How It Works

The Leaderboard tracks community contributions and rewards rebels for their actions. Points are awarded for activities that strengthen the $DEBT ecosystem:

  • Attend a Space: 1 point
  • Host a Space: 5 points
  • Recruit New Holder: 3 points
  • Create Viral Meme: 10 points
  • Major Community Contribution: Variable points

Note: Leaderboard data is manually updated and may not reflect real-time activity. Rankings reset monthly, but lifetime contributions are tracked in the All-Time view.
