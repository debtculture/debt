FAQ

What is $DEBT?

$DEBT is a Solana-based memecoin built as a rebellion against the elite-controlled financial system. Short for "Don't Ever Believe Them," $DEBT is a deflationary token for the 99%, powered by community strength, weekly burns, and a mission to expose systemic lies and corruption.

What makes $DEBT different from other tokens?

$DEBT has no taxes, no team wallets, and no hidden agendas. Launched fairly on Pump.fun with liquidity pool tokens burned and contract renounced, it’s fully decentralized. Weekly burns shrink the supply, and the community drives the rebellion against financial oppression—not just chasing hype.

Why does $DEBT burn tokens?

$DEBT burns tokens weekly to reduce supply, symbolizing defiance against the endless debt creation of traditional finance. Burns aim to increase scarcity and reflect our commitment to a deflationary rebellion, unlike the inflationary systems we oppose.

What is the mission or purpose behind $DEBT?

$DEBT exists to empower the 99% by challenging the elites' control over finance. Through relentless community action, viral memes, and a transparent burn system, $DEBT is building a movement that exposes the broken debt-based system and fights for financial freedom and truth.

What factors might influence $DEBT’s value?

Weekly burns reduce $DEBT’s supply, potentially increasing scarcity, while community growth and engagement on X and Telegram boost visibility. Future utility like staking or DAO participation may add value, but crypto markets are volatile—always do your own research and understand the risks.

What is the long-term vision for $DEBT?

$DEBT is building a lasting legacy through a community-led DAO, real-world utility like tipping and trading, and massive future burns. Plans include NFT integrations, staking opportunities, and strategic partnerships—all aimed at disrupting the broken financial system while empowering $DEBT holders.

How can I get involved with the $DEBT community?

Join the rebellion on Telegram (@debtculture), follow us on X (@DebtCulture), and catch our X Spaces every Monday, Thursday, and Saturday at 9 PM EST. Buy and hold $DEBT, share memes, recruit rebels, or pitch your skills to the team!

What are badges?

Badges are special achievements awarded to $DEBT community members for their contributions and loyalty. They recognize actions like participating in discussions, holding $DEBT, promoting the project, creating memes, or burning tokens. On desktop, hover over a badge to see its description; on mobile, tap to reveal it. Want to see all badges? Check out the Badge Glossary in the Community section!
